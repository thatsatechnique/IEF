Tell Me More

Mission

The Infosec Education Foundation was established to enhance and nurture the global security community. We are committed to expanding awareness of information security and creating education and vocational opportunities through the Brakeing Down Security Podcast, SeaSec East meetup, and the Infosec Campout security conference.

Podcast

Brakeing Down Security Podcast - Interviews with industry leaders and technical experts - 3k downloads/week, 30k/month. 5% annual growth. New content weekly.

Networking Events

SeaSec East Meetup - Instructional talks, networking, career guidance. 20+ monthly attendees, over 550 registered contacts.

InfoSec Campout

Annual camp-based conference. 60 attendees in first year, 6 unique talks, camp-related events and social activities.

About

  • Pre-2019

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Several disparate efforts toward a common goal of facilitating InfoSec education. SeaSec East and Brakeing Down Security Podcast individually run with consistently larger audiences.

  • March 2019

    Foundation Created

    In an effort to centralize efforts and bring together several disparate initiatives, InfoSec Education Foundation was officially formed. This brought together a group of passionate professionals dedicated to furthering InfoSec education opportunities for all. It was clear that these initiatives could gain effeciency by uniting efforts and that creating opportunities to partner with industry would help continue the good work.

  • January 2020

    Looking to the future

    Strides are being made to formalize the foundation's mission and properly staff the Board to meet current needs. Our team is working diligently to get all social media, this page, and other communication methods updated to effectively get the message out. We want to help as many people as possible in this field and that takes a good bit of up-front work. We are currently looking for sponsorships, speakers, and appropriate partnership opportunities. Regular operations are continuing and planning has begun for the next campout!

  • August 2020

    InfoSec Campout 2020

    Join us for our 2nd annual InfoSec Campout. More details to come soon!
